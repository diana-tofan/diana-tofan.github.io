Visual Exploration of Self-Tracking Data
Web application that generates interactive temporal visualizations
given quantified-self data
Overview
Case study of the psychological treatment of PTSD (posttraumatic stress disorder). A patient shares self-tracked symptoms with his therapist and this application delivers visual aids aimed to improve communication and maximize the discovery of crucial insights that can reveal - if put together - the root cause of the problem.
Role & Duration
Software developer | Usability researcher | UX designer (6 months)
Research Question

How to extract meaningful patterns from temporal data with minimum effort and maximum results?
